# Hand Mirror — Quick Camera Check Before Calls From Menu Bar

## About Hand Mirror

Hand Mirror provides instant camera preview directly from your menu bar. One click opens a floating window showing exactly what your webcam sees. Hand Mirror combines one-click access with minimal interface. Hand Mirror supports multiple cameras, resolution adjustment, and mirror mode (selfie view vs what others see). Hand Mirror enables users to check lighting, framing, background, and appearance before joining video calls. Hand Mirror works with any connected camera. Hand Mirror is trusted by remote workers, video conferencing professionals, content creators, streamers, online teachers, and frequent callers worldwide.
